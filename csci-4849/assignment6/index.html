    • Wait for desired key

  • Test results
    • I tested different operations at a 1 second interval compared to a 1.5 second interval
    • 2 + 2
      • 1 second: 30 seconds (average)
      • 1.5 seconds: 46 seconds (average)
    • 64 / 23
      • 1 second: 46 seconds (average)
      • 1.5 seconds: 70 seconds (average)
    • 873 * 235
      • 1 second: 62 seconds (average)
      • 1.5 seconds: 94 seconds (average)
    • Conclusion: I had no errors at the 1 second interval. I think the interval speed could be less than a second and most people would not have problems however I do think 1 second is probably the most accessible interval for the majority.
  • Anything else you have tried out
    • I was satisfied with the interval control because it was the easiest to use in my opinion
